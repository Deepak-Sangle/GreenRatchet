# Product Overview

## Product Purpose
GreenRatchet is a comprehensive platform for managing and reviewing cloud usage with automated environmental impact tracking. It connects directly to cloud providers (AWS, GCP, Azure) to monitor real-time resource consumption, calculate environmental metrics, and provide actionable insights for optimizing cloud infrastructure sustainability and costs.

## Target Users
- **Cloud Operations Teams**: DevOps and infrastructure teams managing cloud resources
- **Sustainability Officers**: Executives tracking environmental performance and KPIs
- **IT Managers**: Teams responsible for cloud cost optimization and resource management
- **ESG Compliance Teams**: Teams responsible for environmental reporting and compliance
- **Engineering Teams**: Developers and architects optimizing AI/ML workloads

## Key Features
- **Multi-Cloud Integration**: Direct connections to AWS, GCP, and Azure for automated data collection
- **Real-Time Environmental Tracking**: AI workload detection, carbon footprint calculation, energy/water monitoring
- **Advanced Analytics & Reporting**: Interactive dashboards, trend analysis, regional analysis, optimization recommendations
- **Comprehensive KPI Suite**: CO2 emissions, GHG intensity, energy consumption, water withdrawal, AI compute hours, renewable energy tracking
- **Cost & Sustainability Optimization**: Identify opportunities to reduce both costs and environmental impact
- **Complete Auditability**: Immutable audit trail with versioned calculations and full metadata

## Business Objectives
- **Cloud Optimization**: Help organizations optimize cloud usage for both cost and sustainability
- **Automation Excellence**: Eliminate manual environmental reporting through automated cloud integration
- **Environmental Impact**: Enable measurable reduction in cloud infrastructure environmental footprint
- **Compliance Readiness**: Support ESG reporting requirements and environmental standards
- **Scalability**: Handle enterprise-scale cloud infrastructure monitoring and reporting

## User Journey
### For Cloud Operations Teams
1. Connect cloud accounts (AWS/GCP/Azure) for automated data collection
2. Set up monitoring for key workloads and resource types
3. Review real-time environmental and cost metrics through dashboards
4. Identify optimization opportunities for sustainability and cost reduction
5. Track progress on environmental KPIs and cost savings over time

### For Sustainability Officers
1. Access comprehensive environmental impact reports across all cloud infrastructure
2. Monitor progress against sustainability targets and KPIs
3. Generate compliance reports for ESG reporting requirements
4. Identify high-impact areas for environmental improvement
5. Track organizational carbon footprint trends and reductions

## Success Criteria
- **User Adoption**: Active organizations using the platform for cloud monitoring
- **Data Accuracy**: Real-time cloud integration providing accurate environmental and cost metrics
- **Optimization Impact**: Measurable improvements in cloud efficiency and cost reduction
- **Environmental Impact**: Demonstrable reduction in carbon footprint and resource consumption
- **Compliance Achievement**: Meeting ESG reporting and environmental compliance requirements
- **Platform Reliability**: 99.9% uptime with enterprise-grade security and performance
